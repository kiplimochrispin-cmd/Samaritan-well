Programs

Community Programs and Water Support Tracks

These programs align faith-centered social action with reliable water delivery, hygiene education, and institutional support.

Program Pillars

  • Household access and refill continuity
  • School and clinic water support
  • Emergency response distribution
  • Training on hygiene and safe storage

Program 1

Parish Household Water Access

Supports vulnerable households through mapped parish routes, targeted delivery schedules, and refill follow-up based on household size and consumption.

Program 2

Schools and Clinics Supply Plan

Provides recurring 20L jar distribution to schools and clinics, with priority for high-footfall facilities and child care institutions.

Program 3

Emergency Drought and Crisis Support

Activates a rapid response mode for short-term water shortages with local distribution points, volunteer coordination, and monitored dispatch logs.

Outcomes

Expected Impact Areas

  • Reduced travel burden to fetch safe drinking water.
  • Better hygiene outcomes in schools and health facilities.
  • Improved continuity of parish social support activities.
  • Stronger community resilience during climate-related shortages.
