About Us

Santa Shepherds Water Initiative

Santa Shepherds Water is a community-centered water access platform aligned with the Catholic Diocese of Eldoret's social and pastoral development priorities. The platform supports reliable access to safe water for families, institutions, and vulnerable groups through local distribution and refill models.

The initiative theme is Faith in Action through Safe Water and Human Dignity. This reflects the Diocese commitment to integrated evangelization and practical social support.

History

Catholic Diocese of Eldoret Background

The Catholic Diocese of Eldoret serves communities in and around Eldoret within Kenya's Rift Valley region. Over the years, diocesan pastoral structures have supported both spiritual formation and social service programs across education, health, and community welfare.

The Diocese currently references leadership under Rt. Rev. Dominic Kimengich, with administrative and pastoral focus tied to Nandi, Uasin Gishu, and Elgeyo Marakwet contexts.

Vision

Instrument of Love, Peace, and Reconciliation

To be an instrument of God's love, peace, and reconciliation while promoting integral human development in all served communities.

Mission

Evangelization with Integral Human Development

To advance evangelization together with practical social action that strengthens household well-being, health, education, and long-term resilience.

Goals

Water Access Program Goals

  • Improve access to safe and affordable drinking water.
  • Support schools, clinics, and parish service points with dependable supply.
  • Promote hygiene and household water safety education.
  • Establish accountable distribution and refill cycles for sustainability.
