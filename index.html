About Us

A local water platform with a calmer, clearer customer experience.

Santa Shepherds Water serves Eldoret with bottled drinking water and refill options for homes, schools, churches, clinics, and offices. The platform is built to reduce the effort required to browse products, place an order, and reach support.

The updated interface focuses on comfort and usability: larger touch targets, stronger spacing, clearer pricing, and sections that adapt cleanly from phone screens to wide desktop layouts.
